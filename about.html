Our Story

Learn More About Us

ST4U Wines is an established wine company founded in 2021, with a growing footprint across South Africa. We are present in Burgersfort, Polokwane, and Vaal, supplying quality wines sourced from superb producers in Stellenbosch.

Our focus is to provide a consistent, enjoyable wine experience to local customers across all provinces, while building a trusted brand known for quality, community and service.

  • Founded in 2021 with a growing national footprint
  • Quality wines sourced from Stellenbosch producers
  • Service-driven approach for customers across SA

Vision

To become a key player in the industry by driving elevated wine consumption and building a national presence in South Africa through strategic growth and partnerships.

Mission

To educate, attract, and grow interest in wine consumption, its socialising culture, and the benefits it offers, while growing the business through strategic partnerships with key industry players.

Values

  • Progressive and forward-thinking
  • Supportive to the development of key talent
  • Honest and creative
  • Strategic and growth-driven
  • Transparent in business dealings
